About Scar


Scar 1

Scar is Simba's uncle. Scar is Simba's evil uncle. Scar was always jealous. Jealous of his older brother Mufasa. And he killed him. As seen when he foolishly admits to killing Mufasa. A trait which proves to be his downfall. As he ultimately pushes Simba too far. Scar is an adult. Adult Lion.

Scar 3
